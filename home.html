Rental Policies

Important information about our rental terms

Age Requirements

Minimum rental age is 21 years. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young renter fee. Some vehicle classes have higher minimum age requirements.

Insurance Options

We offer several insurance options to protect you during your rental. Basic coverage is included, with options to upgrade for additional protection.

Cancellation Policy

Reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before pickup. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Fuel Policy

Our standard policy is "full-to-full." You receive the car with a full tank and must return it full. Prepaid fuel options are also available.
