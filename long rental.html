Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our long term rentals

What's the minimum rental period for long term rentals?

Our long term rentals start at 28 consecutive days. This allows us to offer you significant savings compared to daily or weekly rates.

Can I change vehicles during my long term rental?

Yes! Depending on your rental package, you may have free or discounted vehicle swaps available. Our 3-month package includes 1 free swap, 6-month includes 2 free swaps, and 12-month includes monthly swap options.

Are there mileage restrictions?

No, all our long term rentals include unlimited mileage so you can drive without worrying about extra charges.

What happens if the vehicle needs maintenance?

We cover all scheduled maintenance - just contact us to arrange service at one of our approved facilities. You'll receive a replacement vehicle if yours needs to be in the shop for more than 24 hours.

Can I extend my long term rental?

Absolutely, we're happy to extend your rental period as needed, subject to availability. We'll simply adjust your contract and continue with the same terms or update to reflect a longer rental period discount if applicable.
